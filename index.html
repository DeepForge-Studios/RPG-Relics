Chapter 1

Getting started

Install the pack, grab your starter kit, then walk chapters 2–5: find relics → equip them → build a Boost → attune skills.

Relic Tome

Your pocket guide — Boost Codex, relic catalog, and settings (including Attunement on/off and Standard / OP balance).

Relic Crate

Opens the Reliquary — a portable wardrobe with face, head, neck, rings, back, body, hands, belt, feet, extras, and charms.

Attunement Codex

Browse affinities, skills, and ranks here. Rituals themselves run on the placeable Forge block.

First join includes starter relics plus Relic Dust and an Arcane Gem for early crafts.

Install

  1. Import the .mcaddon and enable Behavior + Resource packs.
  2. Leave Experiments / Beta APIs off (and Cheats off) for achievements.

Chapter 2

Find relics

Relics come from exploring — towers, homes, camps, archaeology, biome mimics, and rare hostile drops. Bring Relic Dust home for crafts and rituals.

Mimics

Biome-tinted chests that awaken and fight. Each variant has its own bite — kill them for Relic Dust and nearby relic loot.

Materials

Focus drops and catalysts — hearts, fangs, gems, herbs, and Relic Dust — for Forge crafts and attune rituals.

Chapter 3

Equip in the Reliquary

Open a Relic Crate to wear what you found. Slots cover face, head, neck, ring, back, body, hands, belt, feet, plus extras and charms — your loadout feeds Boosts and attunements.

Chapter 4

Boost Codex

Each equipped relic adds Boost power by rarity (Common 1 · Uncommon 2 · Rare / Ascended 3). Your strongest style becomes active — tap I / II / III to preview what that tier does.

Chapter 5

Attunement

Study skills in the Attunement Codex, then run the ritual on a placed Forge. Spend Relic Dust plus a matching focus pair to roll an affinity skill onto a relic your Boost style allows.

Attunement Codex

Read affinities, triggers, costs, and ranks — reference only; it does not run the ritual.

Forge

Placeable Craftables block. Put the relic in the bay, pay Relic Dust from your bag, and offer both focus materials.

Chapter 7

Ascended fusions

Fuse two matching relics plus a catalyst at the Relic Forge for a stronger Ascended form. These count as Rare-tier Boost power when equipped.

Codex

Materials

Catalysts and ritual fuel. Relic Dust pays Forge costs; focus materials steer which affinity you roll; Arcane Gems show up in crafts and upgrades.

Codex

Mimics

Six biome-tinted chest mimics. They can awaken from deep or camp chests, and they tint to the land around them. Fight them for Relic Dust — relics often drop from nearby structure loot.
