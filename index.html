Chapter 2

Find relics

Relics drop from exploration — towers, camps, archaeology, mimics, and rare kills.

Mimics

Fake chests that bite. Defeat them for relic loot.

Materials

Hearts, fangs, dust, herbs, and shards fuel crafts and attune rituals.

Chapter 3

Equip in the Reliquary

Use the Relic Crate. Slots cover face, head, neck, ring, back, body, hands, belt, feet, extras, and charms.

Chapter 4

Boost Codex

Equipped relics add power by rarity (Common 1 · Uncommon 2 · Rare/Ascended 3). Your strongest style becomes active.

Chapter 5

Attunement

Learn skills in the Codex. Run rituals on the Forge block — shards + focus materials roll an affinity skill.

Codex

Skills, affinities, ranks, triggers.

Forge

Placeable block for the actual ritual.
